AROGYAM

INDIGESTION REMEDYS


1. Ginger
|| Ginger is an excellent remedy for indigestion.
It can be munched in slices or taken in tea.
It secretes digestive juices and enzymes to help you digest those stubborn fats in your food.
2. Tulsi
|| Tulsi or basil leaves are known for their ability to drive away
intestinal gases and reduce bloating. Take about 5-6 leaves, wash
them properly and then make a paste out of it. Add it with a pinch of salt
and pepper in 2-4 table spoons of yoghurt. Consume 2-3 times a day.
3. Saunf
|| Roast/Grind a handful of saunf seeds (fennel ) and consume it with water,
daily after meals for a healthy digestion. Alternatively, the seeds can be chewed
after meals for a quick remedy.
4. Ajwain
|| Carom seeds also known as ajwain are traditionally used to cure indigestion.
Take a spoon full of it and chew it after a heavy meal.
It can also be grounded and mixed with dry ginger.
